Getting listed

Your menu, sitting next to the dining halls.

Students already open UPlate to decide where to eat. Getting your restaurant into that decision takes three steps, and we do two of them.

1

Send us your menu

A PDF, a link, or a photo of the board. Whatever you already have on hand is enough to start.

2

We build it out

We add every item along with its nutrition information, so students can see your food the same way they see dining hall food: calories, macros, and which restrictions it clears.

3

Students find you

You show up when someone filters for gluten-free, searches for something high in protein, or is just tired of the dining hall and wants somewhere close.

Why UPlate

Everyone else knows what students clicked. We know what they ate.

That difference is the whole pitch. Ad platforms infer appetite from behavior that happens on a screen. UPlate records the meal itself, because students log it.
